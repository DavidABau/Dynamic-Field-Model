- emotional reactivity  

Stress waves typically contract inward.

Long-term waves that unfold over months or years.

Characteristics:

- broadening of accessible archetypes  
- increased intelligence integration  
- more stable access to high-complexity modes  

Developmental waves reflect the natural growth of the cognitive system.
